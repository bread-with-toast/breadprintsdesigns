What is this?

BreadPrintsDesigns is an online store dedicated to selling 3D printed items. You can find the online store either through this link or on the site.

What is 3D printing?

3D printing is a form of additive manufacturing which uses plastic. A nozzle is heated up then plastic goes through it and melts as it is placed on a hot surface.
